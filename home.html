BrainPalmSeq

Curated RNA-seq database of palmitoylating and depalmitoylating enzymes in the mouse brain Browse the expression patterns of palmitoylating and depalmitoylating enzymes, and their accessory proteins, curated from numerous published RNAseq studies across a variety of brain regions and cell-types. For an overview, “Search by gene” plots expression for each gene across selected whole-brain RNAseq studies. For detailed visualization, “Search by brain region” and “Search by cell-type” plot expression across hundreds of cell types in the nervous system. Use the menus to toggle between studies and the interactive heatmaps to select genes, regions or cell-types of interest. These plots can be used to interactively compare expression across all genes and cell types within each study.

What are the effects?

Methods: Siletti et al 2023 Cell-types: All Brain regions: Whole brain Techniques: Human postmortem tissue acquired from donors with no known history of neuropsychiatric or neurological conditions were considered for inclusion in the study. Single cell RNAseq with 10x Chromium. 3,369,219 from 105 dissections.Cells assigned to 31 superclusters, 461 clusters, and finally 3313 subclusters. Expression per cluster is displayed in BrainPalmSeq heatmap. Age of donors: Male and female donors 18 to 68 years of age Data processing for BrainPalmSeq: Single-cell counts were downloaded from cellxgene, and log normalized by first scaling the expression values provided to a sum of 10,000 per cell before calculating log2(scaled_counts+1). Averages were then performed by cell type taxonomy for each gene. Genes associated with palmitoylation were selected in order to create the heatmaps. Heatmap units: mean log2(counts per 10,000 + 1) References: Kimberly Siletti et al., Transcriptomic diversity of cell types across the adult human brain. Science382,eadd7046(2023).
